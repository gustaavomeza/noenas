Our projects

Burfa Capital is a commercial organization, the company has been operating since 2013, emphasizing collaboration and investment in such priority areas as IT, fintech and data processing.

SCROLL

Burfa tech

A leader in the field of data center design and maintenance for the industrial sector, Burfa Tech specializes in high-performance computing and turnkey data center solutions. Using a broad range of construction systems, Burfa Tech implements the projects of any scale or complexity. In October 2019, Burfa Tech was awarded first place in the ranking of the most successful IT companies in Estonia created by Estonian financial newspaper Äripäev.

Burfa media

The company provides computing equipment for processing large data arrays and for any operations that require significant computing power. In addition, Burfa Media offers digital marketing and PR services as well as technical expertise to ensure flawless hardware performance.

Burfa real estate

The company is engaged in the construction of commercial and residential luxury real estate in Estonia for the subsequent sale and rent. Our licensed engineers and specialists conduct a full analysis and coordinate construction projects as well as maintain the finished objects.

Polybius

A fintech company developing OSOM, a comprehensive money management service that allows the users to analyze transactions and control their financial services and assets.

Advendor

A leading affiliate network offering solutions in the various fields of CPA marketing. Founded in 2015, the company has so far generated over 4 million leads and ensured stable profits for both publishers and advertisers. At the moment, Advendor has over 200 CPA offers in gaming, finance and dating industries.

Cyberland

Cyberland develops easily scalable software solutions for both new and existing business products. The company creates tailor-made agile products and tests experimental concepts under real conditions to reduce the likelihood of financial losses.

Apico

The company is engaged in full-cycle software development: from a concept to a working product. At the moment, Apico is designing a mobile app Lokimo — self-guided city tours and quests.

Lokimo

Lokimo is a mobile app with self-guided tours created by experienced guides and locals. Unlike classic city tours, Lokimo routes take the travelers off the beaten path, let them move at their own rhythm and cost less. Developed by Apico in 2019, Lokimo currently has over 300 tours and quests in more than 60 cities worldwide, from Tallinn to Sidney.

Nuland

Nuland is a crowdsourcing platform that connects people to their communities at the grassroots level. Nuland fosters local circular economies and enables positive and valuable interactions between people, their communities, and the environment.

Using the Sustainable Development Goals as a benchmark, Nuland combines community with the blockchain to support a transition to greater sustainability.

Codiv Ukraine

Codiv Ukraine builds high-load cloud systems in the fields of blockchain and internet marketing using the most innovative technologies and programming languages. lt also specializes in the development of mobile applications for Android and iOS.

We want to make
a world of difference

Innovators at heart and one of the fastest-growing companies in Estonia.

We dare to implement world-changing ideas that disrupt the local market and go far beyond it.

With our entrepreneurial mindset and exceptional technical expertise, we aim to become a recognized market leader and set trends and standards for IT services and solutions.

Burfa Capital is headquartered in Tallinn, Estonia with three more offices in Belgium, San Marino and Ukraine.

Our story

  • June 2013
    Founded by two entrepreneurs from Ida-Virumaa who aimed to put Estonia on the global tech map with their large-scale hardware solutions.
  • February 2015
    Foundation of Advendor, a leading affiliate network offering solutions in the various fields of CPA marketing.
  • June 2017
    Polybius Foundation, a subsidiary of Burfa Capital, became the first company in Estonia to issue ICO tokens and introduce the concept of a token in the Estonian legal field.
  • February 2018
    Our IT department scaled up and became a separate company called Cyberland. The company develops easily scalable software solutions for both new and existing business products.
  • February 2019
    Burfa Capital entered the list of the top fast-growing companies in Estonia, whose work, according to the Estonian financial newspaper Äripäev, strongly contributes to the national economy.
  • October 2019
    Burfa Tech was awarded first place in the ranking of the most successful IT companies in Estonia created by Estonian financial newspaper Äripäev.
  • Present Day
    While our priority areas are IT, fintech and databases, Burfa Capital also has portfolio companies focused on real estate, digital marketing, software development, and AI/AR.

What is important for us?

We are people-first

Burfa Capital is a people company with family values. Since 2013, we have expanded from a team of five like-minded individuals to over 100 people in four countries. Venturesome, honest and open-minded, our employees grow together with the company over the years and remain our most valuable asset.

At Burfa Capital, we learn by doing and appreciate ideas and innovations. We respect those who challenge the status quo, take responsibility and aim to get better at everything they do. We regularly attend conferences, business forums and various specialized industry events, as participants, speakers and sponsors.

We remember our roots

Our founders Ivan Turygin and Sergei Potapenko come from Estonia, a country with 1,3 million citizens and four unicorn start-ups. As Burfa Capital scales up and expands geographically, we keep investing in the future of our home country and people living next to us.

In 2019, our subsidiary Burfa Tech in cooperation with Enefit Technology Park completed the construction of the largest computing center in Northern Europe. It will provide the customers with cloud computing, graphical rendering, artificial intelligence and machine learning services.

We create opportunities for the youth

As an innovative company, Burfa Capital participates in sTARTUp Days and collaborates with the Tallinn Technical University to hire the brightest minds. Creative and independent young professionals cooperating with experienced seasoned employees help us create successful solutions and companies.
